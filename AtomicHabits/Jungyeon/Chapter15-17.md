## THE FOURTH LAW : MAKE IT SATISFYING

### Chapter 15: The Cardinal Rule of Behavior Change
- We are more likely to repeat a behavior when the experience is satisfying
- The first three laws of behavior change increases the odds that a behavior will be performed this time. The fourth law increases the odds that a behavior will be repeated next time

##### THE MISMATCH BETWEEN IMMEDIATE AND DELAYED REWARDS
- immediate-return environment(wild animals) vs. delayed return environment (modern day human)
- You are walking around with the same hardware as your Paleolithic ancestors
- The brain's tendency to prioritize the present moment means you can't reply on good intentions.
- Time inconsistency : The way your brain evaluates rewards is inconsistent across time. You value the present more than the future.
- The brain's tendency to prioritize the present moment means you can't rely on good intentions.
- Cardinal Rule of Behavior Change : What is immediately rewarded is repeated. What is immediately punished is avoided.

##### HOW TO TURN INSTANT GRATIFICATION TO YOUR ADVANTAGE
- Immediate reinforcement can be especially helpful when dealing with habits of avoidance.
	- It can be challenging to stick with habits like 'no alcohol this month' bc nothing happens when you don't drink.
	- One solution is to open an account and transfer small amount of money every time you keep your habit.
- It is important to select short term rewards that reinforce your identity rather than ones that conflict with it.
	- e.g. reward for exercising can't be to eat a bowl of ice cream


### Chapter 16: How to Stick with Good Habits Every Day
- Visual measurement provide clear evidence of your progress.

##### HOW TO KEEP YOUR HABITS ON TRACK
- Don't break the chain is a powerful mantra.

- Benefits of Habit Tracking:
	1) Habit tracking is obvious
	- Habit tracking keeps you honest. Most of us have distorted view of our own behavior.
	2) Habit tracking is attractive
	- The most effective form of motivation is progress. Visual proof of your hard work motivates you to continue your progress.
	3) Habit tracking is satisfying
	- Crossing off items itself becomes its own form of motivation
	- Helps you to focus on the progress not the end goal

- But, tracking habits itself can also feel like a burden
	- used automated data then have weekly, monthly review sessions
	- Only use manual tracking for your most important habits
	- record each measurement immediately after the habit occurs

##### HOW TO RECOVER QUICKLY WHEN YOUR HABITS BREAK DOWN
- Never miss twice
	- If you miss one day try to get back into it as quickly as possible
- The first mistake is never the one that ruins you. Its the spiral of repeated mistake that follows.
- When successful people fail, they rebound quickly.
- Another problem people face is an all-or-nothing cycle. The prob is not slipping up but thinking that if you can't do sth perfectly, then you shouldn't do it at all
	- Lost days hurt you more than successful days help you
- Doing sth for 5 min doesn't improve your performance but it reaffirms your identity.

##### KNOWING WHEN (AND WHEN NOT) TO TRACK A HABIT
- Goodhart's Law : Humans are excellent at optimizing for what is rewarded. Meaning one could end up choosing a wrong measurment.
	- Choose a measurement that gives you mores signals of progress


### Chapter 17: How an Accountability Partner Can Change Everything
- Increase the speed of the punishment associated with the behavior. There can't be a gap between the action and the consequences.
- When relying on punishment to change behavior, the strength of the punishment must match the relative strength of the behavior it is trying to correct.
- The more local, tangible, concrete, and immediate the consequence, the more likely it is to influence individual behavior.

##### THE HABIT CONTRACT
- Habit Contract : a verbal or written agreement in which you state your commitment to a particular habit and the punishment that will occur if you don't follow through. Try to actually sign it off with one or two people to act as your accountability partner.
- If you don't want to create a full blown habit contract, simply having an accountability partner is useful.
- People care about the opinions of those around us bc it helps if others like us. This is why getting an accoutability partner or signing a habit contract can work so well.
